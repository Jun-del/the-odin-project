Mushroom Spinach Omelet

Easy to make breakfast: Mushroom Spinach Omelet

A simple and light omelet.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Shredded cheddar and parmesan cheese
  • Salt, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes
  • Chopped fresh mushroom, onion, and fresh spinach

Steps

  1. Whisk egg substitute, Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes together in a bowl.
  2. Heat olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat; cook and stir mushrooms and onion until tender, 4 to 5 minutes. Add spinach; cook until spinach wilts, 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Pour in egg mixture; swirl pan around to evenly distribute egg mixture. Cook until egg is fully cooked and set in the middle, 5 to 10 minutes. Cut into wedges.

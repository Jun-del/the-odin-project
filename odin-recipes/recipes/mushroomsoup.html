Hungarian Mushroom Soup

Hearty and conforting Hungarian Mushroom Soup

A simple and healthy Mushroom soup that derives a lot of its flavours from other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Mushroom (heart and soul)
  • Onions and garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Creme fraiche or sour cream
  • Chopped parsley
  • Chicken or vegetable broth (for vegetarian)
  • Seasonings (E.g., dried thyme, sweet Hungarian paprika, black pepper)
  • All purpose flour

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Saute the onions in the butter for 5 minutes. Add the mushrooms and saute for 5 more minutes. Stir in the seasonings: dill, paprika, soy sauce and broth. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk the milk and flour together. Pour this into the soup and stir well to blend. Cover and simmer for 15 more minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Finally, stir in the salt, ground black pepper, lemon juice, parsley and sour cream. Mix together and allow to heat through over low heat, about 3 to 5 minutes. Do not boil. Serve immediately.
